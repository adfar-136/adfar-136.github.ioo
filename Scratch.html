Game Development With Scratch

What will your kid learn?

In Game Development program for kids, your child will learn to:

  • Code without writing a code.
  • Create their own stories, games, and animations.
  • Think creatively in their own coding ideas.

What are we going to build together in this course?

First Project:

Animate their name with dancing letters.

2nd Project:

Catching Ball Game.

Third Project:

Dodge Game.

Fourth Project:

Boat Racing Game.

Fifth Project:

Giga and Enemy Game.

Final Project:

Pacman Game.
