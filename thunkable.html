Mobile Development With thunkable

What will your kid learn?

When enrolled in the Thunkable program for kids, your child will learn the answers to questions like:

  • What is cross-platform?
  • What is the Thunkable platform?
  • What are the different phone sensors and how they are used in the applications?
  • How do you use and implement different platform components to achieve a project idea?

What are we going to build together in this course?

First Project:

We will work on creating different basic apps, such as color-changing backgrounds, alarms and maps. This will help the student understand different phone sensors and how to design an app interface.

2nd Project:

We will work on creating a to-do list application so we can learn about database, how to create it, what it is used for, and how we can integrate with the Thunkable platform. We will then learn how to implement a data list viewer to show a userâ€™s data.

Third Project:

We will work on understanding the concept of speech recognition and how a mobile app can understand our speech. We will then learn about what a virtual assistant is, and build a basic virtual assistant.

Fourth Project:

We will work on understanding the concept of text-to-speech, how a phone can talk to us, and about Y translator. We will then use the previous knowledge of speech recognition to build an app that listens to our speech and translates it.
